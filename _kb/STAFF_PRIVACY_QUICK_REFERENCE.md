## Privacy Reminders

### What Happens to Photos?

**Approved:**
- Photo deleted automatically **7 days** after approval
- Customer can order again without re-verification for 7 days

**Rejected:**
- Photo deleted automatically **30 days** after rejection
- Customer added to blacklist (if fraud suspected)
- Full refund processed

**Abandoned:**
- If no review within 30 days, photo auto-deleted

### Who Can See Photos?

**ONLY:**
- Age verification reviewers (with permission)
- IT Manager (for technical support)
- Security Lead (for audits)
- Director (for compliance)

**Your Access is Logged:**
- Every photo view is recorded
- Includes your staff ID, IP address, timestamp
- Logs retained for 7 years (compliance)
